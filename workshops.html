Workshops Artisans

House of the Faun offers classes for everyone from beginners to experts.

The majority of the workshops offered fall into the categories of Craft, Textiles and Functional Art such as weaving, bookbinding, natural dyeing, or a variety of other creative practices. Lead by experts in their field, these intimate classes teach traditional techniques that are somewhat lost in modern society.

Our workshops are a form of self-care; an opportunity to gather together in a nurturing and inspiring environment that sparks self-discovery. Explore artisanal techniques that honor the time it takes to make something by hand. Questions? Contact us
